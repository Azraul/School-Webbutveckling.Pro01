Stay awhile and listen

When you develop a character like Cain, old, wise and humble and have voice actor record the line “Stay awhile and listen”, are you perhaps trying to relay more than just a memorable quote in a video game or was it all unintentional.

Art is the emotional expression of human personality.
- Véron, Eugène (1882). L'Esthetique (1st ed.). Paris. p. 35.

Unintentional or not as the artist tries to convey his or hers message it is up to us, the recipient, to decide how we act upon that message. What do you feel when you hear “Stay awhile and listen”.

If you want to hear Deckard Cain click here or on the header above to hear the voice from all those years ago.

As technology advances so does out society and at a faster pace each year and for me the words remind me that sometimes one should see what’s in this moment here and now, rather than always looking for the horizon.
