About

Kristoffer Kuvaja is a 28-year-old male student at Arcada University of applied sciences, residing in Finland, Espoo with his wife and 7 year old son.

The project was made during his first year of studies for the course Webb-development. The goal of the site is to promote himself and his passions while learning the basics of html5 and CSS.

Many of the sites elements where inspired by various sites across the internet but was mainly built with the many resources available for free at: https://www.w3schools.com/
